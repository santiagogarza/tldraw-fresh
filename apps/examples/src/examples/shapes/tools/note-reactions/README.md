Add emoji reactions to sticky notes. Hover a note to reveal the reaction button, pick an emoji, and see who reacted.

---

Hover an empty note to reveal a smiley button. Click it to open the emoji picker. Once reactions exist, a condensed pill shows up to three emoji badges and expands on hover to list who reacted. Reactions persist between sessions when using a persistence key.
