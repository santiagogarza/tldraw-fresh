Add emoji reactions to sticky notes with hover-revealed pickers and persisted reaction pills.

---

Hover a sticky note to reveal a reaction button in the lower-left corner. Pick from five preset emojis to react. Reactions appear as a compact pill on the note and expand on hover to show who reacted. Click an emoji again to remove your reaction. Reactions persist across reloads when using a persistence key.
